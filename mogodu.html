Mogodu

Prep time: 45 mins


Price: R79

Yeild: 4 serving

Ingridients









Steps

  1. Clean up the tripe well and chop it into smaller size as you prefer. wash well
  2. put the tripe into a saucepan and add your onions,salt,ginger,pepper and stovk cubes
  3. add some water to it, cover with the lid and cook for about 2 hours or until the tribe is tender. stir occasionally while it is on fire and add more water when necessary.
  4. When your mogodu is ready, drain any left over stock from it and serve
