Fatcake

Prep time: 40 mins

Yeild: serves 7

Price : R3 each

Ingridients







Steps

  1. Cover the dough balls and allow them to rise in a warm spot (10 minutes)
  2. Mix lukewarm water,sugar, and yeast in a small bowl. let stand until yeast softens and begins to bubble slightly, about 5 minutes
  3. Sift flour and salt together in a large bowl.
  4. Pour water mixture over flour mixture and knead until dough is smooth and elastic, 5 to 7 minutes. cover bowl with clean cloth and let the dough rise until doubled in volume for about 45 minutes.
  5. Roll until smooth and at least 1 inch thick.
  6. heat the oil in the deep fryer or large saucepan to 175 degrees celcius
